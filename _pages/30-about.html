I am an experienced learning leader, technology consultant, project manager, digital product owner, solution architect, communicator, website designer, musician and aspiring travel writer.

With over 30 years within education, learning & development and technology, I've got a huge amount of experience and knowledge to offer to my clients.

I'm an advocate of open-source solutions, like Moodle, where appropriate, but will happily use proprietary software when that would be the better answer.

I was an early adopter of Moodle, and have used it pretty much continuously for the past 20 years. It's extensible architecture makes it a good answer for many (but not all) learning and assessment delivery problems.

I'm fully registered and insured in France, and work with clients across the English-speaking world.

Mark is a highly experienced and customer focused consultant with deep technical knowledge of his subject area. He has worked with a really wide variety of clients and is able to tailor his approach to each one. He provided me and my team with consultancy and practical support which enabled us to review and plug gaps in our online learning provision.
