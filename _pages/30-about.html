I am an experienced technology consultant, project manager, digital product owner, solution architect, communicator, website designer, musician and aspiring travel writer.

  • I help vendors to navigate the learning technology market, design new products, and act as a solutions consultant and implementation manager.
  • I help their buyers get the best out of existing technologies, design solutions, find new suppliers where necessary, and manage projects.

Whilst I am confident in working with the full range of learning technology and knowledge management software platforms, I am a particular expert in the open-source platform, Moodle.

I have been involved with Moodle since its very early days, and implemented it in many organisations. The most recent being the CABI Academy - taking it from a standing start to supporting more than 28,000 learners worldwide.
