I work with mission-driven organisations whose success depends on learning, knowledge, and professional expertise.

I help these organisations make confident decisions about technology by understanding their problems, gathering evidence, challenging assumptions and developing practical strategies for change.

Philosophy

Technology should never be the starting point.

Good technology decisions begin with understanding the organisation, its people, its processes and the problems it is trying to solve.

My role is to help organisations understand their current reality, challenge assumptions and make confident, evidence-based decisions that deliver lasting value.

Organisations come to me for independent advice on digital strategy, learning and organisational change. I help them make better decisions.

I will remain independent.

Principles

  • Start with the problem, not the technology
  • Seek evidence before proposing solutions
  • Challenge assumptions respectfully
  • Consider people, processes and technology together
  • Present options, not predetermined answers
  • Build confidence through clarity
  • Leave organisations more capable than when I arrived

I don't create dependency. I leave behind better thinking.
