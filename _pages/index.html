I help knowledge-intensive organisations make confident decisions about technology.

Philosophy

  • Technology should never be the starting point.
  • Good technology decisions begin with understanding the organisation, its people, its processes and the problems it is trying to solve.
  • I don't create dependency. I leave behind better thinking.

Principles

  • I start with the problem, not the technology
  • I seek evidence before proposing solutions
  • I challenge assumptions respectfully
  • I consider people, processes and technology together
  • I present options, not predetermined answers
  • I build confidence through clarity
  • I leave organisations more capable than when I arrived
  • I am independent

About me

I've worked across public, private and third-sector organisations for over 30 years - at the intersections of learning, technology, knowledge and business.
