Hundreds of companies offer software designed to support teaching and learning in some way or other.

Yet there are some that stand out for offering flexible and highly capable systems - able to work well in a variety of situations.

For many contexts, I recommend organisations look at Moodle and Learndash (based on the popular Wordpress content management system). Combine either of these with the H5P interactive content tool, and the Arlo Training Management software and you will have an extremely powerful system.

For certain, neither Moodle nor Learndash will always be the right answer. There are a multitude of alternatives, for all sizes of organisation, and even for specific industries.

Starter questions

Whenever I'm helping people find the right system, I tend to first ask these five questions. The answers to them will help to quickly narrow down the choice.

What is the business problem you want to solve?

How will you know you've succeeded? What measures will you use?

Who are you providing a service for?

Is it just people in your organisation? If so, do they need to be organised in a certain way to match your organisation structure?

Is it your organisation's customers? If so, are they all individuals, or are your customers also organisations themselves?

How many people will use the system?

Will you be starting small and scaling gradually? Will you need to be able to handle sudden peaks in usage?

Do you have a budget that can handle a per user license fee? How will you be financing the ongoing usage of the system?

How will people get to your courses/resources?

What is your ideal process? Will it be self-service? Do you need card payments? Will there be some form of automation based on what you know about the people?

Will you need some form of single-sign on based on your organisation's or your customers' systems?

What types of learning experience do you want to provide?

Fully online? Mixed with face-to-face? Self-study? Instructor-led? Rigorous assessments? Portfolios of evidence? Branching scenarios? Searchable resources?
