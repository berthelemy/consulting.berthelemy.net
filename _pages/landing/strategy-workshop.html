Organisations regularly reach inflexion points where they need to make decisions about their future direction.

  • Do we start this new project?
  • Do we enter a new market?
  • Do we launch a new product?
  • Do we expand?
  • Do we contract?

At this point, it can be helpful to bring in someone to provide guidance on that direction.

That's where I can help.

With over 30 years in corporate learning & development and not-for-profit education programmes, I bring a wide range of expertise and experience:

  • Learning solution leadership
  • Technology selection and implementation
  • Product management
  • Team leadership

Book me for a strategy workshop with your key stakeholders to help explore your options.

Strategy workshop plan

Preparation

Please be prepared to answer the questions:

  • Where are you currently at, as an organisation?
  • Where do you want to be?
  • What is driving the change?
  • What have you tried already?
  • What has worked?
  • What hasn't worked? Why?

Timing: Before the workshop

Workshop

An online*, interactive workshop with up to seven people.

  1. Introductions & welcome
  2. Your current state
  3. Your inflexion point
  4. Your future state

Timing: 3 hours

Report

I will provide a report of our discussions, along with my proposals for how you should proceed.

Timing: Within 3 working days of the workshop

* I can travel if required. I tend to work online in order to reduce costs for my clients and to maximise my time.

Return on Investment

Without input from an expert, you will probably spend several weeks investigating options, understanding the language being used, and identifying the advantages and disadvantages.

I can help you shortcut that process, thus saving you several thousand euros or pounds!
