Skills Frameworks are an essential part of any work to develop the skills of large groups of people.

They help organisations and individuals to identify potential areas for improvement, and to create appropriate interventions.

Some skills frameworks will be limited to only internal use. Others, generally through publicly-funded organisations, need wider adoption to realise their benefits. With the rising use of AI, alongside search engines, it’s essential that any content is easily readable by Large Language Models - as well as by humans.

This website provides a reference model for organisations to develop and publish skills frameworks. It is designed to be easily modifiable by the adopting organisation, with minimal technical knowledge.
