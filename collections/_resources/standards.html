When we talk about standards, we're considering two types:

  1. Interoperability standards - allowing multiple systems to work together without needing custom-built interfaces.
  2. Quality standards - setting a bar against which to assess organisations and their processes.

For inclusion in the list below, the standard must play some part in a learning technology ecosystem.
